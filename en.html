Key Values & Core Features

A comprehensive automated solution designed to grow your business.

1. Smart Scheduling

The system recommends nurses based on location, rating, and experience. Zero risk of schedule clashes.

2. Real-Time Monitoring

Monitor nurses from departure to GPS check-in, up to the upload of photo evidence for medical services.

3. Patient Medical History

Patient complaints and favorite nurses are securely stored. Customers never have to repeat their stories when re-ordering.

4. Financial & WA Automation

Payment links (Down Payment/Full) and schedule reminders are sent automatically via WhatsApp. Nurse commissions are calculated instantly.

Connecting the 3 pillars of your business automatically in real-time.
